About Staged Launcher

Staged Launcher provides a streamlined interface for developers, testers, and teams to quickly access and launch different versions or configurations of their applications. Say goodbye to complex scripts and manual setup for each stage (like development, testing, staging, production). Built natively for macOS, ensuring a smooth and integrated experience.
